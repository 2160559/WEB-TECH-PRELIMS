What we do

As advocate of cultural heritage and preservation, we would like to encourage and convinced all the people that will be visiting our website to recognize, learn and preserve our own culture. We know that a lot of people is not knowledgeable about their own culture. Because of this future generation might not know about our colorful past that make us successful today. Our website will be focusing on promoting learning and preservation of our own culture.

Who we are and our purpose

Our team is composed of college students taking up either Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) or Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) in Saint Louis University, Baguio City. As Louisians, it is our core values to be creative and socially involved. We created a website to serve the purpose of being an advocate. The main purpose of this website is to give information to the people about how important our culture to us and how culture can help us to make a better future. We will also discuss our own possible solution on how people will recognize and help us preserve our culture.
