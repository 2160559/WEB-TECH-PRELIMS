WHAT WE DO

As advocate of cultural heritage and preservation, we would like to encourage and convinced all the people to recognize, learn and preserve our own culture. We know that a lot of people is not knowledgeable about their own culture. So we decided to make a webpage that full of information about how important our own culture. To this we choose Barangay San Vicente of Baguio City, in this barangay we will look how the barangay preserve and passed their culture, generation to generation. The information that we gathered in the barangay will be our basis to all the information that in this webpage.

WHO WE ARE

Source: Pexels.com photo by Fancycraves

Our team is composed of college students taking up either Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) or Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) in Saint Louis University, Baguio City. As Louisians, it is our core values to be creative and socially involved. As a student we know that culture is important to us and we are aware that some people is not knowledgeable about their own culture. Because of this future generation might not know about our colorful past that make us successful today. So as a group we decided to make a webpage that advocate culture preservation.
