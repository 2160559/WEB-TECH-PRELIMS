WHAT WE DO

As advocates of cultural heritage and preservation, we encourage everyone to join us in our mission to strengthen the cultural foundation of Filipinos. We create a website that highlights the importance of culture. In line with this, we chose Barangay San Vicente of Baguio City in order to look into how the barangay preserve and pass their culture from one generation to another.

WHO WE ARE

Source: Pexels.com photo by Fancycraves

Our team is composed of college students taking up either Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) or Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) in Saint Louis University, Baguio City. As Louisians, it is our core values to be creative and socially involved. That is why we created a website that will enlighten the future generation about the past which made the future colorful.
